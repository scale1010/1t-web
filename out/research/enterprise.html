FAQ (security & operations)

Will users see documents they can't access?

No. Retrieval respects source permissions; cross-workspace access is blocked by default.

Can we restrict or prioritize sources?

Yes—policy supports allow/deny lists, trust weighting, and redaction rules.

How do you handle data retention and legal holds?

Per-policy retention with hold flags; audit events and exports preserved accordingly.

Can we replay a past answer for audit?

Yes—deterministic replays with the exact retrieval set, prompts, and parameters.

Do you support on-prem/VPC?

SaaS by default; VPC/private routing and customer-managed keys are available.
