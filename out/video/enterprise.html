Frequently asked questions

How do you ensure brand control across all video content?

Our brand-style engine automatically applies your typography, motion graphics, and voice timbre to all content. Combined with approval workflows, this ensures 100% brand consistency.

What legal review checkpoints are available?

Role-based approval flows with tracked reviews, policy checks for PII and disclaimers, and immutable audit trails for complete legal oversight.

How is data retention handled?

Zero-retention mode available with customer-managed keys. Data residency controls and DLP policies ensure compliance with regional requirements.

What indemnity options are provided?

Enterprise customers receive comprehensive DPA coverage and indemnity options as part of our SOC2/ISO compliance framework.

Which LMS platforms are supported?

Native integrations with Cornerstone, Workday, Blackboard, Moodle, and SCORM/xAPI compatibility for any LMS.

How does watermarking work?

Dual watermarking system with both visible and invisible watermarks for content protection and tracking across all distribution channels.
